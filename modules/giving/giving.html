Where it goes

Every dollar has a name on it.

We translate dollars into outcomes — not vague impact statements. Here's exactly what your gift unlocks at each level.

    One week of school meals

  • $100

    Through Rise Against Hunger — 100 meals delivered to families facing food insecurity in our partner communities.

  • $500

    Full materials for a scholarship student — books, supplies, and a laptop loan if needed.

    One full year of masterclass access and coaching for a student in our Equip program — the same playbook that built Mognetize.

  • $5,000

    One student's tuition plus a full Earning Blueprints curriculum and coaching track — from classroom to first income.

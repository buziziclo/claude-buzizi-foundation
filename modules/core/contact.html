Frequently asked

Quick answers before you write

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes. Buzizi Foundation is a registered nonprofit. You'll receive a tax receipt with our EIN within 24 hours of any gift.

How is your money allocated?

100% of restricted gifts go to the program you designate. General gifts cover both program work and minimal operating costs — detailed in our annual report.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes — most of our work is digital, including masterclass facilitation and ambassador programs. See the Get Involved page.

How do I apply for support?

Use the Claude Application form under Resources, or send us an email with your situation.

Our Mission

To be a source of warmth — feeding, teaching, equipping — and to gather around it those kept furthest from it.

Claude Buzizi Founder & CEO
A note from the founder

"This Foundation is a promise I made to my late father — and to every widow, every child, every family he set out to help."

My father, Claver Buzizi, started a non-charity project in his village of Bisesero (Kibuye) after the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi — quietly helping widows and families in need. I was born in Rwanda, did my schooling through P3 in Nairobi where the Maasai taught me resilience, and later moved to Belgium where I finally built the income engine I needed to carry his work forward.

The Foundation takes that promise global. The only success worth keeping is the kind that gathers.

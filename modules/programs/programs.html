Programs

Three pillars. One flywheel.

We don't run a long list of programs. We run one engine — Feed, Educate, Equip — where each pillar funds the next, and every dollar moves through people who were once on the receiving end of it.

In partnership with

The organizations and ecosystems that fund and power our flywheel.

01

Feed — in partnership with Rise Against Hunger

Meals delivered through Rise Against Hunger's network into communities facing the highest rates of food insecurity. Every dollar directed here is matched against RAH's $0.05-per-meal cost — so $1 puts 20 meals on a table. No overhead skim, no warehousing. Direct, packed, shipped.

Partner · Rise Against Hunger Mechanic · $1 = 20 meals Funded by · Earning Blueprints + donors
02

Educate — scholarships and masterclass access

Tuition support and masterclass seats for underserved youth — the kid in the village or the neighborhood who has the talent but not the doorway. Recipients gain access to the same curriculum and coaching infrastructure that built Mognetize Marketing, alongside their formal schooling.

Coverage · Tuition + masterclass seat Curriculum · Earning Blueprints + coaching Geography · East Africa & US
03

Equip — coaching that turns recipients into givers

The exact playbook Claude used to escape 13-hour factory shifts in Rwanda — Earning Blueprints, masterclasses, and one-to-one coaching — placed in the hands of people in our programs. The goal isn't a handout that ends. It's an income stream that lets today's recipient become tomorrow's funder.

Format · Masterclass + coaching Curriculum · Earning Blueprints Outcome · Income, not dependence
How the flywheel turns

Income built on the Mognetize side feeds the Foundation. The Foundation feeds, educates, and equips. The equipped become the next generation of givers.

When you subscribe to Earning Blueprints for $1, we donate 20 meals through Rise Against Hunger in your name. When you give to the Foundation directly, you choose which pillar to fund. Either way, the same conviction holds: your success should not stop with you.

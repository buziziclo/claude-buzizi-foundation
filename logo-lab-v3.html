Option 01

CBF Nested Lockup

The C wraps the entire mark like a sheltering arc. The B sits inside it, two stacked bowls. The F locks onto the B's right spine — three letters reading as one solid form. A faint outer ring whispers the sun motif without competing.

Why it works: Reads as a unified institutional seal at any size. The interlock is what makes it feel earned — like a foundation, not a startup.

Option 02

CBF Linked Chain

The C and B literally share a vertical stem — they are one letter doing two jobs. The F sits as a sibling on the right at the same scale and rhythm. Reads horizontally like a wordmark of three glyphs.

Why it works: The shared stem is the visual idea — "linked" not as a ribbon but as architecture. Most legible of the three at very small sizes (favicon, social avatar).

Option 03

CBF Sun Seal

The CBF lockup is reverse-knocked out of a solid disc — the source of warmth from the origin story, now carrying your initials. The disc reads as the sun first, the letters second. Most "institutional crest" of the three.

Why it works: Bridges the Gathering origin story (the source disc) and the institutional CBF monogram. Looks great as a stamp, a wax seal, an embossed wordmark on a report cover, a coin.

My recommendation

Option 02 (Linked Chain) — most legible at small sizes, most "wordmark-like."
